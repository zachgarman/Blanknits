At Blanknits

We specialize in designing and knitting blankets which are personalized to your specifications. We pride ourselves on creating one-of-a-kind pieces of art. We don't re-use patterns; at Blanknits, each blanket is designed from a blank canvas. Whether for baby showers, weddings, anniversaries, graduations, retirements, or any other celebration, our blankets will be sure to add even more smiles to an already joyous event!

What goes into a Blanknit?

Depending on the product you wish to produce, our blankets are made from either 100% acrylic yarn or 90% wool, 10% nylon yarn. All our blankets are warm, soft, durable, machine washable and dryer safe. All our blankets are produced in the USA. Want to learn more?
