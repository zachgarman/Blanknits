The Blanknits Difference

We are a completely unique type of blanket-design company. We don't use specific patterns or recycled designs. At Blanknits, our designs all start from scratch in order to create truly one-of-a-kind works of art! Our creative process is extremely personal; we utilize your color scheme and special occasion and we work with you in order to create the perfect design.

Our Product Lines

We can create designs for just about any celebration or event, and we have two different lines of blankets that utilize different types of yarn to suit your taste. All of our blankets, regardless of the type of yarn used, are machine-washable and durable.

The Stork Blanknits

Our Stork Blanknits are made with 100% acrylic yarn. Warm, soft, and machine washable, they are the ideal gift for any occasion. Each order is uniquely styled to be the perfect, one-of-a-kind blanket that will be treasured for years to come. They can be styled with your two colors of your choice in pattern that is unique and personalized for you.

The Lamb Blanknits

Our Lamb Blanknits are made with 90% wool and 10% nylon yarn. This means you can machine wash and dry this blanket without fear of shrinkage or felting. The main color is natural (or off-white) and the secondary color is a hand-dyed gradient from red to gold to green to blue. This creates an amazing and beautiful one-of-a-kind blanket. The wool we use is very soft and does not cause itching.
