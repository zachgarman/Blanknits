Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of yarn do you use?

For the Stork Blanknits, we use 100% acrylic yarn. For the Lamb Blanknits, we use 90% wool and 10% nylon yarns. They are both very easy to care for because they can be machine washed and dried. Both styles are incredibly soft and warm and will not cause itching. Our yarns come in a variety of colors, so be sure to check out our Colors Page to see for yourself.

What is the design process like for the Stork Blanknits?

The process differs depending on the type of Blanknit you order. For the Stork Blanknits, you would first choose two colors and a size for the Blanknit. In the comments section, you can describe what you would like the Blanknit to look like. We will customize a new design and email you a preview of it. If any changes need to be made, we will be happy to revise the design up to two times with no additional charges. Once we agree on a design, the Blanknit will be created and mailed promptly.

What is the design process like for the Lamb Blanknits?

For the Lamb Blanknit, there is no need to choose from two colors. Our Lamb Blanknits start as natural, off-white, yarn that is 90% wool and 10% nylon. They are then hand-dyed in a gradient color scheme that ranges from red to gold to green to blue. Just let us know if you would like more or less of each dye color on the finished Blanknit. Be sure to check out some of our Lamb Blanknits designs in our gallery.

How long does delivery take after ordering a Blanknit?

Because of the unique design process, orders typically take between 3 and 4 weeks for delivery. If you need it sooner, we do offer a rush delivery for an additional charge. Please contact us immediately to arrange the special rush delivery.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind our products with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If there is any problem, please contact us immediately. After the blanket is returned, we will give a full refund. Unfortunately, if any of the information provided by the customer is incorrect, we cannot offer a refund, so double check!

What kind of payment can I make?

We accept PayPal as payment. You can visit the Order Page to submit payment. Once we receive your payment, we will design your blanket and send you a design sheet.

Do I get a discount if I buy more than one Blanknit?

If the blankets are identical, there is a 10% discount on the second blanket. Because each is blanket is made individually, we cannot give discounts on each unique blanket.
