About Our Blanknits

The Stork Blanknits

Our Stork Blanknits are made with 100% acrylic yarn. Warm, soft, and machine washable, they are the ideal gift for any occasion. Each order is uniquely styled to be the perfect, one-of-a-kind blanket that will be treasured for years to come.

The Lamb Blanknits

Our Lamb Blanknits are made with 90% wool and 10% nylon yarn. These blankets are uniquely hand-dyed to create a blanket unlike any other. Machine washable and soft, the Lamb Blanknit's quality is matched only by its distinctive appeal.
